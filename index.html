Customer Support
Can I modify my order after placing it?
You may be able to modify your order if it hasn’t been processed yet. Contact our customer support team as soon as possible for assistance.
How do I initiate a return?
Log in to your account, select the order you wish to return, and follow the return instructions. Ensure the item is in its original condition.
How can I unsubscribe from the newsletter?
Scroll to the bottom of any newsletter email and click the "unsubscribe" link. You can also manage your preferences in your account settings.
Do you offer exchanges for products?
Yes, we offer exchanges for eligible items within the specified return period. Please contact customer support for assistance with exchanges.
How can I place an order on Klothink?
Ordering is easy! Simply browse our website, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your details and complete your purchase.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. Use this number to track your order on our website or the carrier’s site.
What is your shipping policy?
We offer standard and expedited shipping options. Delivery times vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen.
Are there any additional fees for returns?
There are no restocking fees, but return shipping costs are the customer’s responsibility unless the return is due to an error on our part.
How do I create an account on Klothink?
Click on "Sign Up" on our website, enter your details, and follow the prompts to create your account. It’s quick and easy!
Can I change my account information?
Yes, you can update your account information at any time by logging into your account and navigating to the settings or profile section.
Are my personal details secure on Klothink?
Absolutely. We use advanced encryption and follow strict data protection protocols to ensure your personal information is safe and secure.
